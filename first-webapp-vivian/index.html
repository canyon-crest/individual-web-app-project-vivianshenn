Dance Styles

Ballet

Ballet is a classical dance form known for grace, precision, and technical skill.

Hip-Hop

Hip-hop is energetic and expressive, rooted in street culture and freestyle movement.

Contemporary

Contemporary blends ballet and modern dance with emotional storytelling.

Jazz

Jazz is an energetic and sassy dance style characterized with body isolations, sharp dynamic movements, and syncopated rhythms.
