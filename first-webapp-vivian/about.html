Why Dance?

Dancing allows people to build flexibility, strength, coordination, balance, and creativity. Through dancing, people can learn how to express their emotions and personal feelings without words, but through unique body movements that convey different meanings.

There are multiple different styles of dance, all which have their own special history and origin, and different techniques and visuals. Dancers aren't limited to one style, they have a vast variety of styles that give them the opportunity to explore and experience themselves, allowing them to dance and shine on stage the best.
